Settings

The settings page allows for easily updating and making changes to your user profile.

Navigation:#

To navigate to the settings page, hover your mouse over the side navigation bar and select the settings icon.

Update Profile Name:#

To update your user profile name, enter your new user profile name in the Name text field and select the update button.

Update Profile Email:#

To update the email associated with your user profile, enter your new email address in the Email text field and select the update button. This will trigger a confirm email address link to be sent to your newly entered email address that you MUST confirm before being able to access the Tracker Web Portal again. You will also be logged out and required to log back in with your new email address once it is confirmed from the confirm email address link.

Change Password:#

To change the password associated with your user profile, enter your new password in the Change Password text field and in the Confirm Password text field. The password entered MUST match in both the Change Password and Confirm Password text fields. Passwords MUST also conform to our Password Standards.
